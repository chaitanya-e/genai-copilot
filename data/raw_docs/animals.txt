Cats are small carnivorous mammals known for their independence and playful nature.  
They have been domesticated for thousands of years and are popular pets worldwide.

Dogs are loyal and social animals, often called "man's best friend."  
They have been bred for various purposes such as hunting, herding, and companionship.

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their intelligence and strong social bonds.  
They live in matriarchal groups and can communicate over long distances using infrasound.

Birds are warm-blooded vertebrates with feathers, wings, and beaks.  
Many species are capable of flight, while others like penguins and ostriches are flightless.

Tigers are powerful predators and the largest members of the cat family.  
They are solitary hunters and are known for their distinctive orange coats with black stripes.
